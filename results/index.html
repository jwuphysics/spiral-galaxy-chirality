Spiral Chirality · Results

Method

The network gives each image one score. We score the image, we score its mirror image, and we subtract the second score from the first. A mirror turns an S shaped spiral into a Z shaped spiral, but it does not change how a blob, a tilted disk, or noise looks. So everything except the winding direction cancels out in the subtraction, and the sign of the answer is the prediction. During training the network also sees each galaxy rotated by random angles, so the answer does not depend on how the galaxy happens to be oriented.
